History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small developer meetup in San Francisco. Over the years, it has grown into a premier global tech conference attracting thousands of innovators, entrepreneurs, and tech leaders from around the world.

Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and learning in the tech community. TechCon provides a platform where future-shaping ideas are born and shared freely across the global stage.
